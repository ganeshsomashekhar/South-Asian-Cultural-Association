Who Are We?
Welcome to the Allen High School South Asian Cultural Association (AHS SACA) website! We are an organization based in Allen, Texas dedicated to making a positive impact on the community through service while offering a community-like space to Allen High School students who are interested in South Asian culture and/or of South Asian descent. We will hold bi-weekly meetings after or before school, and participate in fundraisers and other charitable events. If you have any questions, please feel free to navigate to the "Contact" section of our page and reach out to us!

How to Participate
