How to Join
The South Asian Cultural Association welcomes anyone with an interest in South Asian culture and/or of South Asian descent. The process to join is rather simple. Just join our Discord server and change your server nickname to your full name. You can join our discord server by either pressing the Discord logo button on the top right of the page, or by clicking the Discord logo right below this paragraph. If you don't have Discord, don't worry! Just reach out to one of us through the "Contact" page and we'll be happy to still include you in the organization!
